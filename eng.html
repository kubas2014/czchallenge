CZ Challenge 2014.

What are we going to do?

We have traveled a lot but we still have a feeling that we do not know our country, Czech Republic, enough!

We came up with the idea that we will travel the Czech Republic on a bicycle and decided to make this trip happen in June 2014. It will be a journey of discovery, self-awareness and fun.

Our goal is to cover approximately 2,200 kilometers with an elevation of 12,500 m in 30 days while the aim is to “copy” the Czech border as much as possible.

We do not want to just pedal this route, but moreover want this expedition to be enjoyable, fun and awesome!

- Jakub and Matěj

SUUNTO

Navigation sits at the core of a successful challenge. With the Suunto SportWatches, we will be able to not only follow the planned route, but also to record it. We are grateful to Amersports that provided us with Suunto watches for testing.

GSI Outdoors

GSI Outdoors equipped us with practical outdoor dishes, thanks to which we will be able to deliver amazing culinary creations even in the toughest conditions. Cheers!

Drift

The whole journey will be shot on the Drift cameras! With all kinds of mounts, you'll see us from every possible angle and thanks to the Drift App, the best shots will be uploaded right from the journey so that you won't miss out on anything!

Belkin

Belkin provided us with some cool accessories for cycling! Our electronics will be greatly protected in the cases a thanks to powerful batteries, we won't run out of juice. Thank You!
